Red XIII, also known as Nanaki, is a playable character in Final Fantasy VII. He is a unique, lion-like creature with a fiery mane and a deep connection to the Planet. Raised by his grandfather, he seeks to uncover the truth about his heritage and protect the world from harm. Red XIII is known for his wisdom, strength, and fierce loyalty to his friends, embodying themes of legacy and environmental stewardship.
