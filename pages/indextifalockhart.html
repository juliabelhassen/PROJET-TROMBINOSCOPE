"The best way to find yourself is to lose yourself in the service of others."

Tifa Lockhart is a prominent character in Final Fantasy VII and its related media. She is a skilled martial artist and the owner of the 7th Heaven bar in Midgar, which serves as a base for the eco-terrorist group AVALANCHE. Tifa is known for her strong sense of loyalty and compassion. Throughout the story, she plays a crucial role in supporting her friends and battling against the corrupt Shinra Corporation.
